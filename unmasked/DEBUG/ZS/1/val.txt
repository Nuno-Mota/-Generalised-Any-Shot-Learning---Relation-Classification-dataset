The Cartesian product is named after René Descartes , whose formulation of analytic geometry gave rise to the concept , which is further generalized in terms of direct product .
René Texier ( 28 October 1882 -- 10 October 1967 ) was a French sports shooter who competed in the 1912 Summer Olympics , in the 1920 Summer Olympics , and in the 1924 Summer Olympics .
Marq Mellor ( born March 5 , 1968 in Long Island , New York ) is a former field hockey forward from the United States , who finished twelfth with the national team at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta , Georgia .
Thomas Coe was a British water polo player who won a gold medal in the 1900 Summer Olympics .
Athanasia Tsoumeleka ( Greek : Αθανασία Τσουμελέκα , ( aθanaˈsia tsumeˈleka ) , born January 2 , 1982 ) is a Greek race walker , who won a gold medal at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens .
Aarzemnieki ( from the Latvian word `` ārzemnieki '' , meaning `` foreigners '' ) are a Latvian band that represented Latvia in the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 in Copenhagen , Denmark , with their song `` Cake to Bake '' .
Martin Urra ( 1931 -- 11 September 2002 ) was a Filipino basketball player who competed in the 1956 Summer Olympics .
On 11 October 2006 DJ BoBo announced in a press conference that he had applied to represent Switzerland at the Eurovision Song Contest 2007 .
Eric Niklas Henning ( born 6 March 1964 ) is a Swedish former alpine skier who competed in the 1984 and 1988 Winter Olympics .
Dimitar Zapryanov ( Bulgarian : Димитър Запрянов ) ( born 27 January 1960 ) is a Bulgarian former judoka who competed in the 1980 Summer Olympics and in the 1988 Summer Olympics .
