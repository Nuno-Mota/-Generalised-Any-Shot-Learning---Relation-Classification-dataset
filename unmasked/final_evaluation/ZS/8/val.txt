Oceansat-2 was launched from Satish Dhawan Space Centre on 23 September 2009 using PSLV - C14 .
Aist 1 was launched aboard the maiden flight of TsSKB Progress ' Volga upper stage equipped Soyuz-2 - 1v carrier rocket , from Plesetsk Cosmodrome Site 43 at 12:30 UTC on 28 December 2013 , following a series of delays .
