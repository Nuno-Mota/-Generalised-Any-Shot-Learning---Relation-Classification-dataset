Caitlin Hulcup is an Australian mezzo - soprano , initially beginning her career in that country before beginning to appear on the international stage from 2007 forward .
Phyllis Rosina Raisbeck MBE ( 28 July 1916 -- 23 December 2006 ) was an Australian opera and concert mezzo - soprano singer .
